Anime refers to Japanese animated TV shows, films, and series, known for their vibrant art, unique character designs, and diverse genres like action, romance, and fantasy, while manga refers to Japanese comic books or graphic novels, typically black-and-white and read from right to left. Manga often serves as the source material for anime adaptations, with both sharing similar art styles and storytelling techniques. Popular examples include Naruto, One Piece, and Attack on Titan. Together, anime and manga are globally celebrated for their captivating narratives and imaginative worlds.
